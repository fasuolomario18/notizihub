**Q: Can I apply for incentives if I'm self-employed?**

Documentation must clearly demonstrate your income level relative to program thresholds.

**Q: Are used electric cars eligible for 2026 incentives?**
A: The 2026 program primarily supports new vehicle purchases, though certified pre-owned electric cars less than two years old may qualify under specific conditions. Used vehicles must carry manufacturer warranties, documentation of previous maintenance, and certification of battery health status.

**Q: What happens if I exceed the income threshold by a small amount?**
A: Applicants slightly exceeding income limits may qualify for reduced incentive amounts rather than complete exclusion. The program implements graduated thresholds preventing sharp benefit cliffs. Contact your regional automotive incentive office for specific calculations based on your circumstances.

**Q: Can incentives be combined with manufacturer promotions?**
A: Government incentives can typically be combined with manufacturer-offered discounts and dealer promotions, significantly reducing your effective purchase price. However, the vehicle's final price cannot fall below certain minimums, and some manufacturer programs may have restrictions. Consult with dealerships regarding specific compatibility.

**Q: How long does the incentive disbursement process take?**
A: After application approval, incentive transfers typically process within 30-60 days. The funds transfer directly to your bank account rather than being applied at purchase, meaning you'll initially pay the full vehicle price before receiving reimbursement.
